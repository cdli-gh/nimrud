Room B

Room B is divided into its respective north, east, south and west walls. The four entrances
B-a, B-b and B-c, B-d and B-e, have been extended into the walls at their respective positions
(B-e being the only entrance without assigned orthostats or bulls). Preserved panels or
fragments of panels are indicated, and hyperlink to associated CDLI web pages (composite
pages together with entries for the physical artifacts).
